<p>This raccoon isn't the first to hitch a ride on the rails, back in December&nbsp;<a href="http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/raccoon-go-train-1.3355372">a "downtown freeloader" raccoon was found on board a Lakeshore West GO train</a> headed from Union station to Aldershot in Burlington.&nbsp;</p> 
<p>In that case, the raccoon&nbsp;did stick around for the ride.</p>
