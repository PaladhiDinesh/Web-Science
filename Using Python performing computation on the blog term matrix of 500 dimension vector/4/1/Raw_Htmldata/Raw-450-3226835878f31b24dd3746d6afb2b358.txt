2016 Health Resolutions + Win 1 of 20 $50 Walmart Gift Cards! Ends 2/15
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-7784322470144503671' itemprop='articleBody'>
<i>This post was sponsored by Nature Made&#174; as a part of an Influencer Activation for Influence Central.</i><br />
<br />
<br />
Are you a resolution maker? I think that a lot of people fail at their resolutions because they make them too hard to achieve. I'm going slow this year by <b>resolving to improve my health</b>, which isn't as difficult as you might think. It's just a matter of eating a balanced diet, drinking lots of water, and adding in a few Nature Made supplements where they're lacking. We're just into February now and because I set the bar fairly low, I'm still going strong.<br />
<br />
It may not be the 1st of the year, but it's never too late to start a healthy journey, and 20 readers will each win a <b>$50 Walmart gift card</b> to help get them on the right track for 2016!<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-79g-9N0AsrM/VrPa6l_AcVI/AAAAAAAAJAs/YGy6AJjBnGo/s1600/Nature-Made-HERO.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="419" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-79g-9N0AsrM/VrPa6l_AcVI/AAAAAAAAJAs/YGy6AJjBnGo/s640/Nature-Made-HERO.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<a name='more'></a>If you're wondering which supplements might be right for you, just look at your current health for clues. Are you tired all the time, have lackluster hair and brittle nails, or get sick more often than you should? Perhaps heart problems run in the family? Now is the time to take care of your body so that it will carry your into your later years in good condition.<br />
<br />
I choose Nature Made supplements because they are the nation&#8217;s #1 best-selling brand for quality vitamins, minerals and supplements* to help support nutritional gaps and they are also the #1 Pharmacist recommended supplement brand in 9 categories**. Pharmacists are who we go to for OTC help, and they always know what the best, most effective products are.<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Oj1gt47QucA/VrPa7GpdeMI/AAAAAAAAJA0/7JTO7qIqtEY/s1600/Nature-Made-shop.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="403" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Oj1gt47QucA/VrPa7GpdeMI/AAAAAAAAJA0/7JTO7qIqtEY/s640/Nature-Made-shop.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
I get my supplements at Walmart because they have the best prices and you don't need to wait for a sale to stock up. Look at the prices in the above photo! You would usually see these prices on the "store brand" supplements in other places. The store brand is fine in a lot of cases, but not when it comes to my health. I want the good stuff, and at Walmart, you can get it for less than anywhere else.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jFE7nVdDm4Y/VrPa66ukSZI/AAAAAAAAJAw/wl2CXQ7jRjw/s1600/Vitamin-D.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="426" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jFE7nVdDm4Y/VrPa66ukSZI/AAAAAAAAJAw/wl2CXQ7jRjw/s640/Vitamin-D.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
I am deficient in D, especially in the winter months. The lack of sunlight and always using sunscreen means lack of D production and it makes me feel tired and just blah. So, taking extra Vitamin D is a must for me. I can get it prescribed, but buying it OTC is much cheaper.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-KpSb1letgfk/VrPa5yaXf7I/AAAAAAAAJAo/yh6eLHVRjJg/s1600/B-Complex-Vitamins.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="426" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-KpSb1letgfk/VrPa5yaXf7I/AAAAAAAAJAo/yh6eLHVRjJg/s640/B-Complex-Vitamins.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NotvnaR2uQQ/VrPa57kNxMI/AAAAAAAAJAk/HWvn2XdY0uA/s1600/B12-Vitamins.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="426" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NotvnaR2uQQ/VrPa57kNxMI/AAAAAAAAJAk/HWvn2XdY0uA/s640/B12-Vitamins.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
Something new I've decided to add are B Vitamins. Too often I feel worn out and stressed and B Vitamins can help with cellular energy production and a healthy nervous system. I love that the Nature Made B-Complex has added Vitamin C to help the immune system as well.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jlgnhGN2Lg0/VrPa7U61IyI/AAAAAAAAJA4/2yGhQhgIhUM/s1600/Vitamins-in-hand.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="426" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jlgnhGN2Lg0/VrPa7U61IyI/AAAAAAAAJA4/2yGhQhgIhUM/s640/Vitamins-in-hand.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
Supplements are the easiest way to get your health back on track, especially if your diet is lacking these vitamins and minerals. I've found the easiest way to organize and remember is to get a weekly pill case. You know the ones: each compartment is labeled with a day of the week? It makes it so much easier for me to make sure I take them and see if I missed a day (yes, I have senior moments at 36!)<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-n7O4SKmTNWQ/VrPa5pUEF_I/AAAAAAAAJAg/Whe6kNTDJLc/s1600/3-Nature-Made-Vitamins.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="426" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-n7O4SKmTNWQ/VrPa5pUEF_I/AAAAAAAAJAg/Whe6kNTDJLc/s640/3-Nature-Made-Vitamins.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
Another easy way to help you reach your wellness goals it to join the <a href="http://www.wellnesswithnaturemade.com/"><b>Nature Made Daily Boost</b></a>&nbsp;microsite. They've put together a series of Daily Boosts that are not complicated, don&#8217;t take much time, and are an easy way to help you feel great. I've joined. <b>Will you join with me and take charge of your health this year?</b><br />
