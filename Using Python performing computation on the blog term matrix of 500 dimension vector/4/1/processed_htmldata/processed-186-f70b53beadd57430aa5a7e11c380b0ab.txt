21 lines, 469 stations, 660 miles of track,
5.6 million passengers a day

New York City is one of the few global metropolises without comprehensive
cell phone coverage in its subway
     __________________________________________________________________

The Subspotting project explores the available cell phone reception using a
custom data logger that has traveled all 660 miles of the subway system
     __________________________________________________________________

By visualizing the existing ‘pockets of connectivity’ the project creates a
comprehensive map of the available cell phone reception in the New York City
underground
