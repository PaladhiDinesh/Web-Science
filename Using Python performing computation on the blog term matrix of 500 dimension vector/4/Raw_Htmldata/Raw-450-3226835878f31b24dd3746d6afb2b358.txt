<br />
Are you a resolution maker? I think that a lot of people fail at their resolutions because they make them too hard to achieve. I'm going slow this year by <b>resolving to improve my health</b>, which isn't as difficult as you might think. It's just a matter of eating a balanced diet, drinking lots of water, and adding in a few Nature Made supplements where they're lacking. We're just into February now and because I set the bar fairly low, I'm still going strong.<br />
<br />
It may not be the 1st of the year, but it's never too late to start a healthy journey, and 20 readers will each win a <b>$50 Walmart gift card</b> to help get them on the right track for 2016!<br />
<br />

Are you tired all the time, have lackluster hair and brittle nails, or get sick more often than you should? Perhaps heart problems run in the family? Now is the time to take care of your body so that it will carry your into your later years in good condition.<br />
<br />
I choose Nature Made supplements because they are the nation&#8217;s #1 best-selling brand for quality vitamins, minerals and supplements* to help support nutritional gaps and they are also the #1 Pharmacist recommended supplement brand in 9 categories**. Pharmacists are who we go to for OTC help, and they always know what the best, most effective products are.<br />

<br /></div>

I get my supplements at Walmart because they have the best prices and you don't need to wait for a sale to stock up. Look at the prices in the above photo! I want the good stuff, and at Walmart, you can get it for less than anywhere else.<br />

<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jFE7nVdDm4Y/VrPa66ukSZI/AAAAAAAAJAw/wl2CXQ7jRjw/s1600/Vitamin-D.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="426" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-jFE7nVdDm4Y/VrPa66ukSZI/AAAAAAAAJAw/wl2CXQ7jRjw/s640/Vitamin-D.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
I am deficient in D, especially in the winter months. The lack of sunlight and always using sunscreen means lack of D production and it makes me feel tired and just blah. So, taking extra Vitamin D is a must for me. I can get it prescribed, but buying it OTC is much cheaper.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-KpSb1letgfk/VrPa5yaXf7I/AAAAAAAAJAo/yh6eLHVRjJg/s1600/B-Complex-Vitamins.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="426" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-KpSb1letgfk/VrPa5yaXf7I/AAAAAAAAJAo/yh6eLHVRjJg/s640/B-Complex-Vitamins.jpg" width="640" /></a></div>

<br /></div>

It makes it so much easier for me to make sure I take them and see if I missed a day (yes, I have senior moments at 36!)<br />
