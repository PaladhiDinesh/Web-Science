</header><div class="entry-content" itemprop="text"><div dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-572a2496-4490-b068-9786-2c876acdf484" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">

<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
All opinions are mine alone. #GameDayGlory #CollectiveBias</div>
<p></p>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Come see how easy it is to make a snack stadium for your game day celebration! Building plans and free stadium decoration printables are included in this post.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<img alt="DIY Game Day Snack Stadium #GameDayGlory AD #football #snacks" height="739" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/NxaFppVJKOIta1uYB6LLvY5N8YlryIQJxSdrg_eSep5v61hsP-cIAQQjdMj4Pk88euWAAkkK2ehCTlUkROTHbeEXjHMP2XkHpRjHKeXF4VY2RWHz2Jm-dne-2SoxyXFE_01UO7TV" style="border: none; transform: rotate(0rad);" title="" width="624" /></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
It&#8217;s playoff time and excitement is in the air! We love to have people over to watch games and cheer on our favorite teams. What does every football party need? Great friends and great snacks. This year I decided to celebrate with a fun, DIY snack stadium.</div>
<p></p>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
When I sat down to design the snack stadium, I wasn&#8217;t sure what size to make it. I realized that size hinged on the materials I used. I considered wood, but that would make for a heavy stadium and require painting. You all know that I am a simple kind of gal, so I wanted my stadium to be easy. I decided to use white foam boards. They are light, easy to cut, and make for easy storage. The standard size of a foam board is 20&#8243; x 30&#8243;, so I knew my size limits. I grabbed some graph paper and started designing.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<img height="787" src="https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/s1Q8ccqTDFQM_JZ5tnrTyg2LLv_lBm8SDHMFYbve-Bv2faJCpNBv6MZKIixw4zT9awlG3saJJSvvd0fRXx4UZt0vEDV3IrWSWjk9PTwhyUzK1nwAo0RBlyRWcMZwct3P4TnODUVn" style="-webkit-transform: rotate(0.00rad); border: none; transform: rotate(0.00rad);" width="624" /></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
I began thinking of how I wanted to decorate the snack stadium. I have access to a printer that makes 11&#8243; x 17&#8243; prints, so I created large graphics. I decided to cut them in half so that they can be printed on a standard printer. I am offering both the 11&#8243; x 17&#8243; and 8.5&#8243; x 11&#8243; graphics as a free download so that you can print and decorate your own stadium.</div>
<h3 dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 4pt; margin-top: 14pt;">
DIY Snack Stadium</h3>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
(Finished stadium is 11”18)</div>
<p></p>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Supplies</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
3 pieces of 20&#8243; x 30&#8243; foam board</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
utility tape</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
large straight edge</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
pencil</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
utility knife</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
snack stadium decorations (<a href="https://sites.google.com/a/dianarambles.com/media/home/snack-stadium-decorations" target="_blank">click here</a> for free download)</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
clear tape or glue</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
baker&#8217;s twine</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
wooden skewers</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
toy goal posts and ball</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
SNICKERS® Minis 40oz.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Skittles® XL 41 oz.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
TOSTITOS® Medium Chunky Salsa</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
TOSTITOS® Original Party Size or Scoops Party Size</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Pepsi™ 20 oz. bottles</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<img height="505" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/ptS7xnvvzlLwgAiDAjdO3Kf3HXHrbwqfrknaztKyxCxbSSL4hY-Uui0ZfyF3jwQ9lFxf3jLVtYx3jJd30eUdoIDttsGzmuDTNigzADgLcJrZwWdAfBasJtVYeZOz4NqEGt9c3VfN" style="border: none; transform: rotate(0rad);" width="624" /></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Directions</div>
<p></p>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Using your straight edge, mark the pattern on the foam board. You need the following pieces:</div>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
A (2) 11&#8243; x 3&#8243; (inside end zone)</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
B (2) 18&#8243; x 3&#8243; (inside sideline)</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
C (2) 11&#8243; x 6&#8243; (outsize end zone)</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
D (2) 18&#8243; x 6&#8243; (outside sideline)</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
E (8) 1/2 of 3&#8243; x 6&#8243; (diagonal supports)</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
F (4) 6&#8243; x 6&#8243; (outside corners)</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
optional: X1 (4) 1/2 of 2&#8243; x 4&#8243; (inside brace for end zone)</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
optional: X2 (2) 11&#8243; x 5&#8243; (inside platform for end zone)</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<img height="383" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/5VOVgsvDMrVijl3lJbejawG0iAttcdPJs5U0cpGDWrZ2faJw8xwaPvv60dW4BXLG1loE5uGuqAgGXSpBU6d8VSdWUVh4QmUzrpjuFzFl9DoiRU_K_p2ZYEmmm9zM26e1k-DQVC21" style="-webkit-transform: rotate(0.00rad); border: none; transform: rotate(0.00rad);" width="624" /></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Using your utility cutter carefully cut out the foam stadium pieces along the marked lines.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Cut out stadium decorations.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<img height="395" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/tzjnKSxMqUr7015CQ9YUbVGivfn2zNziER3FTOTA4wlm1alMlJ_5ab8sNWGLykELKpzHNWiNdXl66-XaWE-WIDTrKr14mtjPIT5s8FmIBll9XoTfKlseLuayejhDP16dXZLosxBP" style="-webkit-transform: rotate(0.00rad); border: none; transform: rotate(0.00rad);" width="624" /></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Assemble your snack stadium as follows:</div>
<p></p>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Attach pieces A &amp; C to form the inside walls of the stadium.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Attach pieces E to each corner.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Attach pieces B &amp; D to create the outside walls of the stadium.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Attach pieces F to the outside corners to finish off the stadium structure.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Optional: Attach pieces X1 &amp; X2 for inside platform if desired. We had to trim these slightly for them to fit.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<img height="798" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/n-xf3ZjIjkX0YbkxLzmwsz5ZVxPyqxCwanl63sqdv87vf3y6k4hp_0rwmMJk_UQy47wH-5t7by2WLI5LQ5PyMzUfbc_efFcf9La62Om7sKg4vi3MwS5Gi5w1InvzoAgk5giZ6KGQ" style="-webkit-transform: rotate(0.00rad); border: none; transform: rotate(0.00rad);" width="620" /></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
I used white utility tape along the edges to hold them together. I put it together with small pieces of tape and then went back and used bigger pieces of tape to make the corner edges nicer.</div>
<p></p>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Decorate your snack stadium. I attached the pennants to baker&#8217;s twine and strung it between skewers stuck into the outside edge.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<img height="183" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/j_WM9AYGMBvisf9PWvgWUUV-oJFTrWRRSoCMJBnaCUbjMTVGoh2WELvfmH20ijXXcnmuvKMb4upBcFrLrSs_0Py801Bpt4nwNa50fNgZdGJbParkj2KNa7qs-b_mqI_NJTWXKx4F" style="-webkit-transform: rotate(0.00rad); border: none; transform: rotate(0.00rad);" width="620" /></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Add your snacks and party!</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-left: 12pt; margin-right: 12pt; margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://cbi.as/21ojb" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><img height="445" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/Tktwxtmbk4M1vOTEJZcv90gDeljNCOuPBsg9DQN46dugOz_k9R-McHeedIGI6ora8Ap-GpYqFXlm03fODedNfgXQNRyBYkp9WnEVT3TGwX2UT7EHFjbkLZH-FAC-LgEvgke5FZ08" style="border: none; transform: rotate(0rad);" width="620" /></a></div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Here is how I filled the stadium:</div>
<p></p>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
I created Skittles® Candy Bark* and placed it in the end zone. *recipe below</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
I opened a tall jar of TOSTITOS® Medium Chunky Salsa and placed it in the middle of the sidelines. TOSTITOS® Original Tortilla Chips filled the space on each side of the salsa.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
I opened jar of queso and placed it to the other side of the stadium flanked by TOSTITOS® Scoops.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
SNICKERS® Minis were placed in two corners of the endzone and Skittles® were placed in the other two.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Pepsi™ 20 oz. bottles were placed on the outside corners of the stadium.</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 16px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Football players were created with SNICKERS® Minis and soft taffy candies. I unwrapped red and yellow soft taffy candies and microwaved them for about 3 seconds to soften. I cut each in half, rolled each into a little round helmet shape, and place on top of unwrapped the SNICKERS® Minis. The helmets stuck to the chocolate as it hardened.<img height="524" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/X0vYt1L1iFZDg1i_o1psF6XNwpLI4cGxoIlrsAwaNReBqLtUwMo73KsvqLbbCuHuvJ9d8Y3PrEad4Ck9mJIbBrBeSM-UPQXcSJId-5mUpMrXjELl8MJOnd8w5HMjzQWgWsGKNNXl" style="border: none; line-height: 1.38; text-align: center; transform: rotate(0rad);" width="620" /></div>
</li>
</ul>

<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 4pt; margin-right: 4pt; margin-top: 4pt;">
Skittles® Candy Bark</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
<img height="435" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/z6vyZDcpA0Upfn8CGjHxe6wpIFZ6kA9j4MCZDUvDxSsn0A58sfMBRLiRP47UseLd8ZrqtZh8QXAtQnuM8Z8GPhR28uixFqLqmW-rx_vlqbDfJqiU3AJkYxOMRW49rGHU33m5UoU4" style="-webkit-transform: rotate(0.00rad); border: none; transform: rotate(0.00rad);" width="400" /></div>

<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Ingredients</div>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 14px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Skittles® Candy</div>
</li>
<li dir="ltr" style="font-size: 14px; list-style-type: disc; vertical-align: baseline;">
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 2pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
White Candy Melts</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Instructions</div>
<p></p>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Melt the package of white candy melts according to the package directions.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Pour the melted candy onto a cookie sheet lined with wax paper or a silicone liner.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Generously sprinkle the Skittles® onto the bark.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Place in refrigerator to harden.</div>
<div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
Break into small pieces to create the bark.</div>
