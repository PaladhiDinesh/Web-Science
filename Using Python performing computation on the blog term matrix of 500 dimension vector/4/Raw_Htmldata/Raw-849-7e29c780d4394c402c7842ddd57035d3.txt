<a href="http://visitlondonderry.com/learn/history/" target="_blank" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/outbound/visitlondonderry.com/learn/history/');"><img title="Historical Landmarks" src="http://www.londonderryimages.com/VisitLondonderry/Ad-Widgets/500x62-Ads/i-3xbXN6F/2/X3/%21visit_ad_500x62_landmarks_morrison-X3.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="62" /></a></div>
<p><a href="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/sherry_header_net02.jpg" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/file/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/sherry_header_net02.jpg');"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-55987" title="On the Road with Sherry" src="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/sherry_header_net02.jpg" alt="" width="494" height="182" /></a></p>
<p>First of all you just can’t argue that we live in the very best State in the Country… New Hampshire!  I can feel it in the air and see it in our development that great things are just beginning and to come!  My dream for Londonderry and New Hampshire is that this is a place, state that our children want to stay in, raise their families in and always come back too… and that’s what’s happening now!  Londonderry and New Hampshire are becoming (and in many areas already are) an Inspiration to other Towns and States……How Great Is That!</p>
<p>The “Inspiration” part of this weeks “On The Road With Sherry” happened just yesterday. . .  Tim Beaton who moved into Londonderry not long after me as it turns out, in the early 70’s, called to ask if there was some way he could register to vote.  Now that sounds easy to most of us, but Tim was stricken at a young age with Multiple Sclerosis.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/tim.jpg" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/file/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/tim.jpg');"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-82100" title="tim" src="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/tim-374x650.jpg" alt="" width="374" height="650" /></a></p>
<p>Yesterday I had the honor of meeting Tim at his home and registering him to “Vote” for the first time.  I asked Tim why “now” with wanting to Register?  Tim said that through the years life got in the way… to busy… meant too… all those things that we all say about so many things.  I asked if he had children and he said no… he shared with me that his wife died very young of Lung Cancer.  He lost her in just 4 short months from the day she was diagnosed.  No warning, no big signs early on. Tim went on to explain to me that Donald Trump “Inspired” him to Register to Vote!  How Great is That! Tim loves his track record in business turning things around, his Passion, his energy, and that his top goal is to make America Great Again!</p>
<p>Mr. Trump inspired Tim, but Tim Beaton inspired me!  I so enjoyed our time together and I know we will be seeing each other again!  Here Tim is in this tough situation that would break most of us and he was one of the happiest, most grateful people I have met in a long time.  We talked about the Candidates, Londonderry and even my girls.</p>
<p>On the way to Tim’s house I pulled into a place that I didn’t even know existed in Londonderry the “Leslie C. Bockes Memorial Forest”.  Tim told me that there are trails back there and many people hike our great woods from this location.  It had a nice safe parking area right on Mills Road.   Tim asked me to remind everyone that this beautiful trail is right there.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/park.jpg" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/file/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/park.jpg');"><img class="alignleft size-large wp-image-82101" title="park" src="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/park-433x650.jpg" alt="" width="177" height="266" /></a>You have to “Google” this Trail.  I just stopped typing to you because I had to learn more… Tim really got me thinking about it and honestly I would have just driven right by!</p>
<p>We have another “Gold Mine” right in Londonderry!” The Google Site for this trail is beautiful.  Here’s what I learned….It was started by Reverend Leslie C. Bockes and her family.  She initially started it as a summer camp for inner city youth from Lowell, Massachusetts.   Now it is known as a Tri-Town Forest being shared by Londonderry, Hudson, and Windham.  There are 226 acres waiting for us!  The trail that many people already enjoy is a 3.6 mile loop from the parking area.  There is a beautiful sign posted with information to get you “headed in the right direction” I guess you could say.</p>
<p>The logging road is considered an easy trail and is great for Horse Back Riding, Hiking, Snow Shoeing and Skiing. Remember this is just one of the many trails available in these Tri-Towns.</p>
<p>I can’t believe I had no idea about this!  Thank You Tim!</p>
<p>Now back to the “Politicking” part… In our house right now the 4 of us are loving every minute of living in our Great State where there could not be a better place to be during all of this “Politicking”.  We haven’t decided yet who we are going to support, but we are doing our very best to watch the debates on TV, attend, listen, and read the information that is out there.  We are proud to be “Independent Voters” or as we need to put on the State Registering to Vote form…. “Undeclared”.</p>
<p>In New Hampshire we don’t have a formal Independent party.  Maybe it’s time that changed… We want the best person for the Huge Undertaking at Hand whether they are Republican or Democrat in every office.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/broderick.jpg" onclick="javascript:urchinTracker('/file/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/broderick.jpg');"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-82102" title="broderick" src="http://www.londonderrynh.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/08/broderick-167x250.jpg" alt="" width="167" height="250" /></a>Honestly I like to see how hard they are willing to work, how personable they are, and yes their background is important but if they have backbone and great listening skills they will be smart enough to surround themselves with specialist in each of the vital areas.  President Regan and President Kennedy would be great examples of this in my book anyway.</p>
<p>Well a couple weeks back John and I were coming out of our new found Bagel Place I told you about in Nashua.  It was one of our very early, unshowered, hoping no one would see us drives to the farmers market… Again.  As we walked into the shop “Outside” was standing Mike Broderick who is running for the position of Mayor of Nashua.</p>
<p>Mike was so personable, friendly and sincere that we wanted to stop and learn more about him.  What an impressive guy!  He simply loves Nashua!  He was in our National Guard, he raised his family there; he started and owns his own business in Nashua over 30 years ago.</p>
<p>John and I were “Inspired” by him!  Standing out there on his day off, handing out flyers, taking a chance, putting yourself out there…. Politics are not easy and we all know how nasty they can get.  His Flyer says “As your experienced and committed Mayor, I will work for you to insure a better quality of life for you and your families”.  To me part of that means he wants to keep our young people and families here, too!</p>
<p>Mr. Broderick had a great handshake, looked you in the eye with sincerity… and if we could vote he would have our vote on September 8th in Nashua.  Vote Broderick!  I know you won’t be disappointed.</p>
<p>With a Grateful Heart for the “Gifts” of Our Great State, Londonderry, Meeting Tim, for good men and woman who are willing to stand up for what they believe in with Grace Under Pressure and for all of you….especially “Wayne” and his gift of words.</p>
<p>Happy Happy Birthday to Me “55” Today xoxoxoxo</p>

function sfc_setCookie(c_name,value,expiredays) {
	var exdate=new Date();
	exdate.setDate(exdate.getDate()+expiredays);
	document.cookie=c_name+ "=" +escape(value)+((expiredays==null) ? "" : ";expires="+exdate.toGMTString());
}

function sfc_getCookie(c_name) {
	if (document.cookie.length>0) {
		c_start=document.cookie.indexOf(c_name + "=");
		if (c_start!=-1) {
			c_start=c_start + c_name.length+1;
			c_end=document.cookie.indexOf(";",c_start);
			if (c_end==-1) c_end=document.cookie.length;
			return unescape(document.cookie.substring(c_start,c_end));
		}
	}
	return "";
}
