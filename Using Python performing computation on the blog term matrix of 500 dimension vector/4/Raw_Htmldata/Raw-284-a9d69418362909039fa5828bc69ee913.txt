                                <p>BRONX &mdash; A man was slashed in the face&nbsp;Saturday morning as he left the West Farms Square-East Tremont Avenue subway station after an argument with two other men, police said.&nbsp;</p><p>The 32-year-old victim&nbsp;was slashed in the right cheek with an unknown object just before 4 a.m., police said.</p><p>The attack followed an argument between the victim and two other men, according to authorities.&nbsp;</p><p>The victim was taken to <a href="https://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/places/st-barnabas-hospital/" target="_blank">St. Barnabas&nbsp;Hospital</a>, where he received stitches and was released, police said.&nbsp;</p><p>The <a href="https://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/tags/nypd" target="_blank">NYPD</a> said there is no indication the victim knew his attacker before the&nbsp;assault.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>No arrests had been made as of Monday morning, police said.</p><p>The incident follows a <a href="https://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20160128/union-square/spate-of-random-subway-slashings-has-straphangers-on-edge" target="_blank">series of slashings this year</a>&nbsp;across New York, including multiple attacks in the subway system.&nbsp;</p>
