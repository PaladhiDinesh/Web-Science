<p>So because of the AWESOME VUDU free movie, we decided to make the first one a family movie night &#8211; and no movie night is complete without a movie night snack. That&#8217;s where the Movie Snack Chocolate Cashew Popcorn comes in. It&#8217;s SO easy &#8211; and the pay off? It was so delicious. Everyone gobbled theirs up while watching VUDU on our Smart TV!</p>
<p><a href="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Chocolate-Cashew-Popcorn-4.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-24267" src="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Chocolate-Cashew-Popcorn-4.jpg?resize=1496%2C2256" alt="Chocolate Cashew Popcorn 4" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a></p>
<p>So to make your Movie Snack Chocolate Cashew Popcorn you&#8217;ll need:</p>
<ul>
<li>Popcorn</li>
<li>Milk Chocolate (to melt)</li>
<li>Dark Chocolate (to melt)</li>
<li>Cashews</li>
</ul>
<p>Honestly THAT&#8217;S IT!</p>
<p><a href="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Edited.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-24270" src="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Edited.jpg?resize=1462%2C1993" alt="Edited" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a></p>
<p>Simply do this.</p>
<ul>
<li>Pop the popcorn (air pop works best!). Cover 2 cookie sheets with wax paper and spread popcorn on wax paper.</li>
<li>Add in Cashews (as many or as few as you want).</li>
<li>Melt both chocolates. Then drizzle popcorn and cashews with chocolates.</li>
<li>Let chocolate harden and serve!</li>
</ul>
<p>See I promised it would be easy!</p>
