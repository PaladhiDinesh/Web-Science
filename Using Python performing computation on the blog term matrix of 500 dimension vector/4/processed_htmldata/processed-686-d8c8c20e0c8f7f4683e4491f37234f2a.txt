   Presidential Candidate Donald Trump Town Hall Meeting in Londonderry,
   New Hampshire Republican presidential candidate Donald Trump spoke at
   the Londonderry, New Hampshire, Lions Club. He spoke about his plans
   and goals for his potential presidency, and then took questions from
   those in attendance.
