Check out the newest deal on this Peppa Pig Peppa’s Deluxe House Play Set
with 3 Figures at Walmart!


   Right now you can get this [132]Peppa Pig Peppa’s Deluxe House Play Set
   with 3 Figures for only $22.98! This is great gift idea for any Peppa
   Pig fan. Plus you can get FREE shipping on orders over $50 or FREE
   in-store pick up.

   Kids can hang out with Peppa and George in their house with the Peppa
   Pig Play Set with 3 Figures. It opens to reveal rooms filled with fun
   furniture. Enter into the living room on the bottom floor complete with
   a couch and television se or cook up some fun in the kitchen which
   includes a stove, refrigerator and a sink. Then kids can head upstairs
   to the bathroom where a bathtub waits. Next door in the bedroom of the
   Peppa’s playhouse deluxe is a bunk bed. It includes over 15 play
   pieces, and folds closed for on-the-go adventures. Come over to Peppa’s
   house for a play date that’s full of surprises.

   [133]Peppa Pig Peppa’s Deluxe House Play Set with 3 Figures:
     * Playhouse unfolds to reveal 4 rooms: bedroom, living room, kitchen
       and bathroom
     * 15 play pieces including washing machine, bathtub, fridge, stove,
       barbecue, dinner table, bunk beds and more
     * Includes 3 articulated figures: Peppa, George and Suzy Sheep
     * Material: plastic
     * Stairs connect the two sections of the Peppa house
     * Fold it closed to take it on-the-go
