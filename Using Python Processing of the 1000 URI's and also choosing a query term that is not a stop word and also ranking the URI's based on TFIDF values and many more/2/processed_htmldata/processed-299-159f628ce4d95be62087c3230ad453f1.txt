Side-Swept Bun

   Don a chic look that’s adventurous, yet still refined.

How to get this look:

   1. Refresh your hair with [637]TRESemmé Fresh Start Dry Shampoo to
   absorb any natural oils.
   2. Part your hair on a side of your choice. The side opposite the part
   will ultimately be the side with your bun.
   3. Take a section of hair from the part and use a pin to keep it out of
   the way until it’s needed while you work on the rest of the look.
   4. Next, take the hair opposite the part and comb it out to the side
   while holding it taut. Twist the hair into a low side ponytail and then
   into a bun. Pin it in place. It’s OK if the bun is slightly messy, as
   that gives it a fun, unstructured look.
   5. Now, take the pinned portion of hair from your part and lightly
   tease it. Then drape it across your forehead, tucking it behind your
   ear. Try to create a “sweeping” look. Keep the look in place by
   finishing with [638]TRESemmé Two Extra Hold Hair Spray.
