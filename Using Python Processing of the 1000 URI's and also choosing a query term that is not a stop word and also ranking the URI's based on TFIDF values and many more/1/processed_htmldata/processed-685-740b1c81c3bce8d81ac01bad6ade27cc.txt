Jeb Bush Remarks in Nashua, New Hampshire

   Former Governor Jeb Bush (R-FL), a 2016 Republican presidential
   candidate, spoke at a Rotary Club luncheon at the Nashua Country Club
   in New Hampshire. After his speech he answered questions from audience
   members.
