Interview with Presidential Candidate Donald Trump

   Republican presidential candidate Donald Trump did a brief interview
   with C-SPAN before his campaign rally in Plymouth, New Hampshire. He
   talked about his plans for trade, stopping illegal immigration, and
   other issues.
