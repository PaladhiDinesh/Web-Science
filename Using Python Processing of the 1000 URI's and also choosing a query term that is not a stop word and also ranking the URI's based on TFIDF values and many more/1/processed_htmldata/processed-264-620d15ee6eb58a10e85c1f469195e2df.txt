Belo Horizonte Metro Information

   The Metro service in Belo Horizonte is well known for an ignominious
   reason- it transports commuters from seemingly nothing to nowhere,
   owing to the fact that the network does not run close to any of the
   numerous places of interest, popular neighborhoods and avenues around
   the city. The Metro service has only one line with nineteen stations to
   its credit, some of which have connections to other places by means of
   bus service.
   [INS: :INS]

   As per the proposals of the Government, there will be an extension of
   the Metro service with the addition of two more lines. It forecasts
   that upon completion of the work, the service will witness a jump in
   daily commuter traffic by nearly a million passengers as the network
   will reach a total coverage of forty-four kilometers. Belo Horizonte
   Metro has an annula ridership of 57.42 million as in 2013.
