<p class="MsoNormal">Those shopping in our stores in China see lots of Chinese New Year decorations and traditional foods stocked for this busy time. For readers who aren’t in China, here’s some background on the celebrations. <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Traditional Family Meals<o:p></o:p></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">Before the first day of the first month in the lunar calendar, people all over China travel to their hometowns to unite with their families and decorate their homes in red — a color that symbolizes good luck and joy — and prepare for Chinese New Year celebrations. The night before the Chinese New Year, we prepare a <a href="http://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/collection/chinese-new-year" title="Link: http://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/collection/chinese-new-year" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="0">feast</a> made up of symbolic foods:<o:p></o:p></p>

<ul><li>In Chinese culture, a fish course represents wealth in the future, while peanuts signify longevity and good health.</li><li>Some food symbolism in Chinese New Year dishes is more visual, such as hot pot, which involves simmering meat and vegetables in a round pot at the center of the table. The shape of the pot represents perfection and satisfaction.</li><li><a href="http://travel.cnn.com/shanghai/life/ultimate-guide-chinese-dumplings-466494/" title="Link: http://travel.cnn.com/shanghai/life/ultimate-guide-chinese-dumplings-466494/" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="0">Dumplings</a> are an example of a food with a more historical tie because they resemble the gold currency — Yuanbao — used in ancient China. Today, dumplings are still thought to signify wealth in the coming year and are a delicious treat stuffed with different fillings.</li></ul><aside class="text-center">
    <img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/09a7a40/2147483647/resize/603x%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2F7d%2Fb6%2Fa02840b04f0cab74e00319449e08%2Fchinese-walmart-store.jpg" width="603" height="403" alt="Chinese Walmart food displays" data-size="blog-post-image"></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Celebrations<o:p></o:p></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">Like with New Year’s Eve in the U.S. and other western countries, Chinese New Year involves staying up late. We light firecrackers at midnight, a tradition that dates back to ancient folklore. Though the New Year is a cause for celebration now, legend has it that Chinese villagers used to stoke their fires with bamboo to keep away a terrifying, sharp-toothed monster that arose from the sea at the end of the lunar year to prey on people and livestock. Now, we use firecrackers to celebrate the new year and also scare off any bad luck that might be on the horizon. <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">Celebrations culminate in the <a href="http://www.travelchinaguide.com/essential/holidays/lantern.htm" title="Link: http://www.travelchinaguide.com/essential/holidays/lantern.htm" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="0">Lantern Festival</a>, where people gather to admire the illuminated lanterns (some floating, some carried by children, some fixed as decorations) and guess riddles written on them. On New Year’s Day, people also watch lion dances, in which participants don elaborate, mythical lion costumes that seem larger than life — and eat rice dumplings. <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">One of our family traditions is for children and grandchildren to wish elders in the family good wishes for the new year and, in turn, the elders will give children a red envelope of money for good luck and to buy toys and books.&nbsp; Children often sleep with the red envelope under the pillow to bring good luck throughout the year. <o:p></o:p></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b>The Year of the Monkey<o:p></o:p></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">This year is the <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2016/01/31/world/gallery/year-of-the-monkey-lunar-new-year/index.html" title="Link: http://www.cnn.com/2016/01/31/world/gallery/year-of-the-monkey-lunar-new-year/index.html" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="0">year of the monkey</a>, the ninth of 12 animals in the recurring 12-year Chinese zodiac cycle. People born in the year of the monkey are believed to be energetic, witty and mischievous. I look forward to greeting the year of the monkey surrounded by my family and enjoying the snacks and festivities that come with the celebrations. No matter your Chinese zodiac, may the New Year bring good fortune to you and your family!</p></div>

<article id="art2" class="article-container" data-title="This Store is Helping Reimagine the Supercenter" data-permalink="http://blog.walmart.com/innovation/20160212/this-store-is-helping-reimagine-the-supercenter?reading-list=00000152-b329-d300-a153-bfbbe63b0000">
    <div class="article-header">
        <div class="article-header-inner">
            <a class="article-topic-title category" href="http://blog.walmart.com/topics/innovation" data-cms-ai="1">Innovation</a>
            <h1 class="article-title">This Store is Helping Reimagine the Supercenter</h1>

            <div class="article-byline">
                <div class="article-byline-text">
                    <p class="article-byline-author">By
                        <a href="http://corporate.walmart.com/search?q=Walmart Staff" data-cms-ai="1">Walmart Staff</a>
                    </p>

                    <p class="article-byline-text"></p>
                    <p class="article-byline-date date">February 12, 2016</p>
                </div>
            </div>
        </div>

        <div class="article-lead-media">    
    <img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/5c9a39b/2147483647/crop/8688x3769%2B0%2B1360/resize/1300x564%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2F66%2Fdb%2F7be1c7b546028d6a6e9ff678117f%2Fscan-and-go-checkout-registers-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" class="article-lead-img" data-size="blogPost_1300x564"></div>
</div>

    <div class="article-body">
        <div class="article-content">
            <p>Last year, supercenter #5260 in Rogers, Arkansas, got a
facelift. Added into the refreshed look were several new approaches to
technologies, services, products and layouts, which are currently being tested
with customers. Early reports are positive, but it’s too soon to tell what’s
working and what isn’t. What’s clear: Things that seem straightforward could
show up in new stores or remodels. Store 5260 is simply the first step toward
the supercenter of the future, but it’s critical to informing upcoming tests.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#if6bc0908bd0944369a8770a4f10aaffe{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="if6bc0908bd0944369a8770a4f10aaffe"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/8e9a69a/2147483647/crop/5000x3333%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2Fab%2Fdd%2F8b8a6efe40b592d2c7190de74ebd%2Felectronics-department-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" width="603" height="402" alt="Electronics department in Walmart Supercenter 5260" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Room to Play:</b> The electronics and
entertainment areas have a sleek, modern look that customers say feels very
welcoming and on-trend. “One of the things that we noticed early on as people
walk by electronics is that they stop and look, and then they get drawn
in," said Sherry Curtis-Swenson, the store’s manager.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#i21f345cb2d66483da60f3dda7412c80c{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="i21f345cb2d66483da60f3dda7412c80c"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/8ef2bc6/2147483647/crop/7360x4912%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2F8a%2Fab%2F3bdc55cd4b988c215d8b21a5f4b5%2Fwalmart-produce-department-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" width="603" height="402" alt="Walmart Produce Department in Walmart Supercenter 5260" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>A New Angle on Fresh:</b> A
reorganization (along with improved sight lines and angled aisles) puts berries
— a growing category — in the front of the department. Bananas, already a huge
draw, are toward the back to help lead customers through. Purple signage in
Fresh and throughout the store connects to an increase in organic products.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#ida5c8d5897314a93844701a1a373d90e{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="ida5c8d5897314a93844701a1a373d90e"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/65cd97d/2147483647/crop/3000x2000%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2Fdc%2F07%2F05a292474c6587a9942905351686%2Fwalmart-automotive-department-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" width="603" height="402" alt="Digital menu boards and service signage in a Walmart automotive area" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Car Care, Customer Care:</b> Along
with new digital menu boards and signage in automotive, there’s a comfortable
customer waiting area — furnished with items from <a href="http://www.walmart.com/" title="Link: http://www.walmart.com/" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="1">Walmart.com</a>. Customers can watch TV, enjoy a coffee,
charge their phones, and see their cars being serviced.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#i6e828b1e36dc4dbdb810d9023c46bdfe{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="i6e828b1e36dc4dbdb810d9023c46bdfe"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/c25543f/2147483647/crop/5000x3333%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2F01%2F03%2Ff4beed6241feba85cc8f52fcbc00%2Fwalmart-pick-up-and-services-desk-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" width="603" height="402" alt="In-store pickup and Walmart services share a space at the front of a Walmart supercenter" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Pickup, Up Front:</b> <a href="http://www.walmart.com/cp/1231921" title="Link: http://www.walmart.com/cp/1231921" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="1">In-Store Pickup</a> and Walmart Services share space up front at
Store 5260. It’s clearly marked so customers can find it and get their orders
quickly.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#i809e3c349ab24ce2b412d6d5fbaf7745{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="i809e3c349ab24ce2b412d6d5fbaf7745"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/09d14f4/2147483647/crop/3333x5000%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2Fd8%2F4a%2F6d60e72449018795e85ff37c0ecd%2Fscan-and-go-checkout-stations-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" width="402" height="603" alt="A wide checkout area features double belts to place your items on for faster checkout" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Check Out Your Way:</b> There
are multiple options for checkout. Scan &amp; Go supplies a wand so customers
can scan items as they’re shopping. Hybrid registers can be self-service or
manned by associates, depending on the need. And high-velocity checkouts — where
a cashier scans items while the customer moves through the line to pay — are
more than three times faster than conventional checkouts.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/141fb1d/2147483647/resize/603x%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2Fc6%2F1f%2Fcba2c49c47ff965707127785bf57%2Fbaby-strollers-line-the-baby-department-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" width="603" height="559" alt="Baby strollers line the baby department floor in a Walmart Supercenter" data-size="blog-post-image"></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>One-Stop Baby Shop:</b> The
new baby department combines it all in one space. There’s even a stroller
garage for hands-on tryouts. “Customers love being able to move the strollers
around,” Sherry said.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#i9ca5cdede12747e99979907b1545c7f4{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="i9ca5cdede12747e99979907b1545c7f4"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/64bf91c/2147483647/crop/5000x3333%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2F8c%2Ff4%2F17f9dce942f3bc2565d42130244a%2Fbig-rub-bbq-restaurant-in-walmart-supercenter-5260.jpg" width="603" height="402" alt="Big Rub BBQ restaurant inside Walmart Supercenter 5260" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal"><b>Local Eats:</b> A local food truck
operator, <a href="https://www.facebook.com/bigrubbbq/" title="Link: https://www.facebook.com/bigrubbbq/" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="2">Big Rub BBQ</a>, has
restaurant space in the store, with lots of glass and natural light — and even
seating on an outdoor patio!&nbsp;</p><p class="MsoNormal"><i>Editor’s note: A version of this story originally appeared in&nbsp;</i><a href="https://us.walmartone.com/en/walmart/news/walmart-world/" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="2"><i>Walmart World</i></a><i>, the
magazine for Walmart associates.</i></p></div>

            <div class="article-byline">
                <div class="article-byline-text">

    <div class="article-body">
        <div class="article-content">
            <p class="MsoNormal">My team ended 2015 in a big way. After months of hard work, we opened
the first Walmart store in Nicaragua, offering our everyday low prices on a
wide assortment of 40,000 products – a very significant value proposition for
the Nicaraguan market. We know this to be true because people are welcoming us
with open arms.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/f8ec253/2147483647/resize/603x%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2F67%2F66%2F1a84cc82442fb1c19bdfb4993871%2Fnicaragua-walmart-grand-opening.jpg" width="603" height="400" alt="Hundreds of Customers waiting to enter Nicaragua Walmart during its grand opening" data-size="blog-post-image"></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal">The day we opened, literally hundreds of enthusiastic people attended the
grand opening of our supercenter in Managua, ready to save money on everything
from clothes, electronics and paint to toys, appliances and groceries. Watching
the excitement and knowing that this store is making a difference for these
people reminded me once again of our mission to help people live better by
simply paying less for the things they need.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#ie31a7305fdd54932b0e4bf51ac3422ec{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="ie31a7305fdd54932b0e4bf51ac3422ec"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/4fb2617/2147483647/crop/2144x1424%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2Fd4%2Fff%2F8737debf42e586593958122cb4f0%2Fnicaragua-walmart-clothing-department.jpg" width="603" height="401" alt="Aerial shot of the boys and &amp; girls clothing department" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal">This is a $17 million investment in a modern, comfortable store of
5,890 square meters (over 63,000 square feet) that created 150 direct jobs (the
associates that will work in the supercenter) and 1,575 estimated indirect jobs
(jobs as a result of the supercenter such as cleaning crews and suppliers). And,
because we strongly encourage the growth of local businesses, a great majority
of our assortment comes from small and medium-sized Nicaraguan suppliers. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">Walmart operates 87 stores in Nicaragua under other formats like Palí (discount),
Maxi Palí (warehouse) and La Union (supermarket), but this is the first Walmart-branded
location. We are now offering the distinctive standard of service, price and
assortment through our iconic Walmart supercenter. And the excitement we’ve
seen is definitely our major reward.&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></p></div>

<article id="art5" class="article-container" data-title="The Dish on Gumbaya: A Foodie Follows a Dream" data-permalink="http://blog.walmart.com/us-manufacturing/20160209/the-dish-on-gumbaya-a-foodie-follows-a-dream?reading-list=00000152-b329-d300-a153-bfbbe63b0000">
    <div class="article-header">
        <div class="article-header-inner">
            <a class="article-topic-title category" href="http://blog.walmart.com/topics/us_manufacturing" data-cms-ai="5">U.S. Manufacturing</a>
            <h1 class="article-title">The Dish on Gumbaya: A Foodie Follows a Dream</h1>

            <div class="article-byline">
                <div class="article-byline-text">
                    <p class="article-byline-author">By
                        <a href="http://corporate.walmart.com/search?q=Edward Mueller" data-cms-ai="5">Edward Mueller</a>
                    </p>

                    <p class="article-byline-text">Chief Operating Officer &ndash; Carolina Gumbaya</p>
                    <p class="article-byline-date date">February 09, 2016</p>
                </div>
            </div>
        </div>

        <div class="article-lead-media">    
    <img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/57a5238/2147483647/crop/2409x1045%2B0%2B58/resize/1300x564%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2Ff7%2F26%2F17524c0442b8a7b9c7f9700c2e7d%2Fcarolina-gumbaya.jpg" class="article-lead-img" data-size="blogPost_1300x564"></div>
</div>

    <div class="article-body">
        <div class="article-content">
            <p class="MsoNormal">When I moved from Michigan to Myrtle Beach, S.C., five years
ago, it marked a new beginning for me. I was stepping away from 20 years in the
insurance business, into a warmer climate and yearning to return to my culinary
roots.</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">When I was growing up, my family owned a food processing
plant. I was running a restaurant up north by the time I was 19. I’ve always
been curious about flavors and what’s out there. Whenever I travel, I’m that
guy who <i>only</i> eats local cuisine. And
when I began digging into my new surroundings, I discovered the history of
gumbo in the U.S. – which people naturally associate with Louisiana – can actually
be traced back to South Carolina in the 1600s.</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#i795e3e3ff6844b7f86d881f346ee9de0{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="i795e3e3ff6844b7f86d881f346ee9de0"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/065e158/2147483647/crop/1632x1224%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2Fdf%2Fc8%2Fce94c60c4649836a5acc5bc06a32%2Fcarolina-gumbaya-in-bowl.jpg" width="603" height="452" alt="Carolina Gumbaya is topped with white rice and is in a white bowl" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal">The first recipe I developed when I set foot in Myrtle Beach
was my own gumbo. There were so many beautiful ingredients down here – fresh
shrimp, whitefish, Andouille sausage, okra – and when I dipped my spoon into
that first bowl, I had a moment. I thought, “This is it. I’ve really got
something here.”</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">I knew this was a recipe that would make South Carolina
proud. My gumbo immediately started winning people over at local farmers
markets and festivals. I looked into opportunities to get my product on the
market, from selling to local restaurants to partnering with a delivery service
in the area. But the day the district manager at our local Walmart gave me 15
minutes of his time – that was the day everything changed.</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">That was Dec. 17, 2013. When I walked out 45 minutes later,
it was with the understanding we had a deal. By May 2015, my <a href="http://www.carolinagumbaya.com/" title="Link: http://www.carolinagumbaya.com/" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="5">Carolina Gumbaya</a> was being sold in
the frozen section of 17 Walmart stores in South Carolina. Today, that’s grown
to 137 Walmart stores in five states, including Virginia, North Carolina, South
Carolina, Georgia and Florida.</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">It really has been an amazing experience, to see so many
people embrace this recipe I created in the kitchen of my own home. But when
people ask me if it has taken me by surprise, I have to tell them, “Honestly,
no.”</p><aside class="text-center">
    <style type="text/css">#i78a826b54f0141b6871f95ebc2ed31cc{display:inline-block;overflow:hidden;position:relative;width:603px;height:nullpx;}</style><span id="i78a826b54f0141b6871f95ebc2ed31cc"><img src="http://cdn.corporate.walmart.com/dims4/WMT/6c13fee/2147483647/crop/526x583%2B0%2B0/resize/603x603%3E/quality/90/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fcdn.corporate.walmart.com%2F30%2Fcd%2Fe12297c340abbbddffa643e59a03%2Fcarolina-gumbaya-product-packaging.jpg" width="526" height="583" alt="Carolina Gumbaya product packaging" data-size="blog-post-image"></span></aside>
<p class="MsoNormal">Frozen food has come a long way in recent years. <a href="http://facebook.com/carolinagumbaya" title="Link: http://facebook.com/carolinagumbaya" target="_blank" data-cms-ai="5">Carolina Gumbaya</a> – a name drawn
from the words gumbo and jambalaya – isn’t packed with fillers and
preservatives. The label doesn’t have words you can’t pronounce. There are 12
whole, wild-caught shrimp in every one-quart container. And the blonde roux I developed,
along with my secret spices, are a few of the differentiating factors.</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">Turn on any food channel or open a food publication and
you’re going to hear about the flavor of the South. It’s the South’s time to
shine on the culinary stage – so products like mine have an opportunity to
spread across the country. Along the way, Walmart’s commitment to domestic
manufacturing is opening the door for small entrepreneurs like my business
partner, Laura Spencer, and me. Products like Carolina Gumbaya are helping
create jobs at growing U.S.-based companies like Duke Food Productions, the
company who helps produce our product. These kinds of stories are a win-win for
everyone. And we’re just getting started.<o:p></o:p></p></div>
