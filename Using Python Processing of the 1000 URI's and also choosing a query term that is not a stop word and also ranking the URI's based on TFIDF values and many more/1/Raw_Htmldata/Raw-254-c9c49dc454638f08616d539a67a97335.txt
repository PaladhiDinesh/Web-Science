<p>Along comes <a href="http://www.subspotting-app.nyc/">Subspotting</a>, a new app to help track Wi-Fi service and cell reception for the four major carriers  AT&amp;T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and Sprint  along your daily route. Say you&#8217;re traveling on the Q train from Astoria to Coney Island&#8230;.</p>
