				<p class="western">After reading the first two articles, you are now aware of your emotions and you know how you react in certain situations enabling you to manage your emotions more effectively. The third component to EQ is Social Awareness. We all know someone who consistently offends people without knowing it. It would be safe to say that person has a low social awareness. These are the people no-one wants around in a business environment, at parties or in any situation where relationships are critical.</p>
<p class="western">How socially aware are you? Next time you are with someone, pay attention to the verbal and non-verbal cues they are giving you such as the sound of their voice, the color of their skin, the pace of their breathing, the tension (or not) in their face or other parts of their body. Try to pick up on relationships within groups or the vibe in a room filled with people. Having laser like social awareness enables you to adjust your communication according to the environment, giving you the highest chance of having your voice heard and accepted.</p>
