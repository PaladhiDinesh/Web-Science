Walmart Price Match Recipe: Asian BBQ Pork Sliders

   Walmart Price Match Recipe: Asian BBQ Pork Sliders
   Serves: 8

   Ingredients
     * 1 Boneless Pork Loin Roast
     * 1 Bottle Hickory Smoke or Brown Sugar BBQ Sauce
     * 1 Cup Hoisin Sauce
     * ½ Cup Teriyaki Sauce
     * 1 Tablespoon Minced Garlic
     * Salt & Pepper to taste
     * 1 Package Sister Schubert's Rolls or Similar Yeast Roll/Hot Roll
       you prefer

   Instructions
    1. Place pork loin in roasting pan or slow cookerand lightly sprinkle
       all sides with salt and pepper.
    2. Spread minced garlic over top of pork loin and add 1 cup water to
       bottom of roasting pan or slow cooker
    3. Cook on medium heat (if in slow cooker) for 4 hours. Cook at 375
       degrees for 3-4 hours in oven if using roasting pan method.
    4. While roast is cooking, mix together in small saucepan bbq sauce,
       hoisin sauce, teriyaki sauce and bring to a boil.
    5. Cook for 2-3 minutes and remove from heat
    6. When roast is done cooking use forks and shred leaving in roasting
       pan or slow cooker drippings.
    7. Pour BBQ sauce mixture over shredded meat and mix until all coated
       and jucies from cooking are incorporated into sauce as well.
    8. Heat rolls as directed on package. Slice in half and use as a "bun"

   3.2.1290
   We love these Asian BBQ Pork Sliders served with a simple cole slaw or
   grilled vegetable for an easy and delicious meal. These are great for
   potlucks, parties, dinners with friends or just to serve to your family
   because they have begged for it.
