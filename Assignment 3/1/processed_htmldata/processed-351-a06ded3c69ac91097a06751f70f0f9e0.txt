[12]Walmart Family Mobile Adds New $49.88/10GB Plan To Its Offerings

   Posted by [13]admin 8 February, 2016

   [14]Walmart Family Mobile Adds New Unlimited $49.88 10GB Plan To Its
   Offerings Walmart Family Mobile, a postpaid no-contract MVNO that
   operates on T-Mobile network, has improved its offerings with a new
   plan. Walmart Family Mobile has added a new $49.88 unlimited plan that
   includes unlimited talk, text and data with 10 GB at 4G LTE speeds.

   In addition, the new $49.88 unlimited plan comes with one free Vudu
   movie per month, after each on-time bill payment. This Walmart’s video
   on-demand service is a limited time offer. The movie rental code comes
   in the form of $7.00 Vudu credit that must be used up in 30 days period
   or it will expire.

   It also seems that Walmart Family Mobile has discontinued [15]the
   $24.88 plan that was offering unlimited talk and text only.
   [INS: :INS]

   After the latest change, Walmart Family Mobile’s plans include:
     * $29.88 – includes unlimited talk, text and data with first 1 GB at
       4G LTE speeds
     * $39.88 – includes unlimited talk, text and data with first 5 GB at
       4G LTE speeds
     * $49.88 – includes unlimited talk, text and data with first 10 GB at
       4G LTE speeds (new)

   Data on Walmart Family Mobile plans are reduced to 2G after high speed
   data limit is reached in a month. Also, the plans come with Data Saver,
   well known T-Mobile feature ([16]Binge On or [17]Data Maximizer) that
   provides streaming videos at DVD quality (480p+). Data Saver makes data
   last three times longer than with normal video streaming. The feature
   can be turned off at any time if users want to watch video at higher
   quality. This offer is available for a limited time.

   Customers can add up to five lines with a $5 discount per each line.
   They can activate service on their own phone, T-Mobile compatible or
   unlocked GSM phone, or they can purchase one from Walmart Family
   Mobile. Customers are required to purchase a $25 Starter Kit for each
   line of service. The monthly plans don’t include taxes and fees so
   additional cost of $0.97 Regulatory Programs Fee per line and taxes
   that range from 6% to 28% apply. In addition, off network voice and SMS
   roaming is included while data roaming is not.

   Source: [18]Walmart
