Perseverant Happy Meal refuses to spoil after 6 years

   1.5k
   Shares
   [53]Share
   [54]Tweet
   [55]Share
   What's This?
   Mcdonaldsmeal_thumbnail
   Image: Facebook [56]Jennifer Lovdahl
   [57]2015-09-10%2012.51.40-1
   By Diana Shi2016-02-08 18:00:38 UTC

   Those fries stuck behind your car seat haven't gone bad — but maybe
   hold off on eating them.

   [58]Jennifer Lovdahl purchased a Happy Meal from McDonald's and it's
   still looking almost good enough to eat. The Happy Meal is from six
   years ago.

   See also: [59]Couple stops for finger lickin' good wedding photo in
   front of KFC

   [60]
   Screen Shot 2016-02-08 at 12.37.12 PM

   As part of an experiment to determine how truly durable McDonald's food
   can be, Lovdahl allowed her Happy Meal to sit around her office without
   disturbing it. Fast-forward six years later, and the meal of nuggets
   and a small fry are still looking pretty fresh.

   In the [61]Facebook caption, Lovdahl describes that the meal "has not
   rotted, molded, or decomposed at all ... It smells only of cardboard."

   She guessed the high number of chemicals probably kept the meal in such
   a disturbing condition.

   "The box will decompose before the food does," Lovdahl [62]wrote in the
   description of another photo. "This is disgusting."

   It's a museum-worthy meal for only three bucks and some change. That's
   not bad at all, if you don't mind some mild indigestion.
