   Chicken Sausage McMuffin:

   My boys are 10, 12, and almost 14. They are all at an age where they
   are having growth spurts, and hungry ALL the time, and eat, ALL the
   time. They eat a healthy, hearty breakfast before school each day. They
   eat a hot, healthy lunch at school each day. They also take several
   healthy, filling snacks with them to school, to snack on throughout the
   day. Despite this, when I pick them up in the afternoon, they are
   starving. The oldest two attend the same school, and the youngest
   attends another. They get dismissed about 30 minutes apart, and
   McDonald’s is in between the two schools, off the main road to drive
   between them. Occasionally one of the two older boys will beg me to
   stop and get them something to eat, saying they feel shaky and like a
   headache is coming on, because they are SO HUNGRY. Last week, we had
   one of those days, so I stopped in to let them try the new Chicken
   Sausage McMuffin. We are all excited that McDonald’s now offers
   breakfast all day!

   [44]Chicken Sausage McMuffins

   McDonald’s JUST launched a new breakfast sandwich item on their menu,
   the Chicken Sausage Egg McMuffin.

     A great alternative to our traditional pork sausage, the chicken
     sausage is a juicy breakfast option flavored with rosemary and
     additional spices. It contains 5 grams of fat and 80 calories and is
     available as a substitute protein on any McDonald’s breakfast
     sandwich or platters.

   [45]Chicken Sausage McMuffins

   This is a close up of one of the Chicken Sausage McMuffins that we
   received. It was pretty big, the egg, cheese, and chicken sausage
   completely filled the McMuffin from edge to edge. It was hot, fresh,
   and ready for us to grab while on the go.

   [46]Chicken Sausage McMuffins

   The middle child did not want his picture taken. With Middle School
   kids, some days they love it, other days, not so much :) The each dove
   right into their Chicken Sausage McMuffin the moment we walked in the
   door from school. They all gave it an excited thumbs up.

   [47]Chicken Sausage McMuffins

   All three immediately gave it a thumbs up. The only downside (for me)
   is that now they want one EVERY DAY when we pass McDonalds :) They all
   really enjoyed the sandwich, and when I do stop to pick them up
   something, this is what they request. On the plus side, Chicken Sausage
   is one of the better choices for them to make, and it filled them up. I
   love that they are eager and willing to try new things.
