<p><strong>Chicken Sausage McMuffin:</strong> </p>
<p>My boys are 10, 12, and almost 14. They are all at an age where they are having growth spurts, and hungry ALL the time, and eat, ALL the time. They eat a healthy, hearty breakfast before school each day. They eat a hot, healthy lunch at school each day. They also take several healthy, filling snacks with them to school, to snack on throughout the day. Despite this, when I pick them up in the afternoon, they are starving. The oldest two attend the same school, and the youngest attends another. They get dismissed about 30 minutes apart, and McDonald&#8217;s is in between the two schools, off the main road to drive between them. Occasionally one of the two older boys will beg me to stop and get them something to eat, saying they feel shaky and like a headache is coming on, because they are SO HUNGRY. Last week, we had one of those days, so I stopped in to let them try the new Chicken Sausage McMuffin. We are all excited that McDonald&#8217;s now offers breakfast all day!</p>
<p><a href="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffins.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-7044"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7044" src="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffins.jpg" alt="Chicken Sausage McMuffins" width="528" height="261" srcset="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffins-300x148.jpg 300w, http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffins.jpg 528w" sizes="(max-width: 528px) 100vw, 528px" /></a></p>
<p>McDonald&#8217;s JUST launched a new breakfast sandwich item on their menu, the Chicken Sausage Egg McMuffin.</p>
<blockquote><p><em>A great alternative to our traditional pork sausage, the chicken sausage is a juicy breakfast option flavored with rosemary and additional spices. It contains 5 grams of fat and 80 calories and is available as a substitute protein on any McDonald’s breakfast sandwich or platters.</em></p></blockquote>
<p><a href="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-7045"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7045" src="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg" alt="Chicken Sausage McMuffins" width="546" height="728" srcset="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin-225x300.jpg 225w, http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg 546w" sizes="(max-width: 546px) 100vw, 546px" /></a></p>
<p>This is a close up of one of the Chicken Sausage McMuffins that we received. It was pretty big, the egg, cheese, and chicken sausage completely filled the McMuffin from edge to edge. It was hot, fresh, and ready for us to grab while on the go.</p>
<p><a href="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Testing-out-the-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-7046"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7046" src="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Testing-out-the-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg" alt="Chicken Sausage McMuffins" width="500" height="167" srcset="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Testing-out-the-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin-300x100.jpg 300w, http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Testing-out-the-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg 500w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p>The middle child did not want his picture taken. With Middle School kids, some days they love it, other days, not so much <img src="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" /> The each dove right into their Chicken Sausage McMuffin the moment we walked in the door from school. They all gave it an excited thumbs up.</p>
<p><a href="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Thumbs-Up-for-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-7047"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7047" src="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Thumbs-Up-for-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg" alt="Chicken Sausage McMuffins" width="500" height="167" srcset="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Thumbs-Up-for-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin-300x100.jpg 300w, http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Thumbs-Up-for-Chicken-Sausage-McMuffin.jpg 500w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" /></a></p>
<p>All three immediately gave it a thumbs up. The only downside (for me) is that now they want one EVERY DAY when we pass McDonalds <img src="http://practicalfrugality.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" /> They all really enjoyed the sandwich, and when I do stop to pick them up something, this is what they request. On the plus side, Chicken Sausage is one of the better choices for them to make, and it filled them up. I love that they are eager and willing to try new things.</p>
