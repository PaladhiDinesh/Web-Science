<section class='article-content'>

<p>Those fries stuck behind your car seat haven't gone bad â€” but maybe hold off on eating them.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.facebook.com/balancedhealthchiropracticalaska" target="_blank">Jennifer Lovdahl</a> purchased a Happy Meal from McDonald's and it's still looking almost good enough to eat. The Happy Meal is from six years ago. </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2016/02/08/kfc-wedding/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">Couple stops for finger lickin' good wedding photo in front of KFC</a></p></div>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Screen-Shot-2016-02-08-at-12.37.12-PM.png"><figure class="image"><img data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-caption="" title="Screen Shot 2016-02-08 at 12.37.12 PM" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE2LzAyLzA4LzA5L1NjcmVlblNob3QyLjU1NWM0LnBuZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/dcaa01df/03c/Screen-Shot-2016-02-08-at-12.37.12-PM.png" alt="Screen Shot 2016-02-08 at 12.37.12 PM" class="" data-width="494"></figure></a></p>
<p>As part of an experiment to determine how truly durable McDonald's food can be, Lovdahl allowed her Happy Meal to sit around her office without disturbing it. Fast-forward six years later, and the meal of nuggets and a small fry are still looking pretty fresh. </p>
<p>In the <a href="https://www.facebook.com/balancedhealthchiropracticalaska/posts/10153433398490669" target="_blank">Facebook caption</a>, Lovdahl describes that the meal "has not rotted, molded, or decomposed at all ... It smells only of cardboard." </p>
<p>She guessed the high number of chemicals probably kept the meal in such a disturbing condition.</p>
<p>"The box will decompose before the food does," Lovdahl <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10153433396535669&amp;set=pcb.10153433398490669&amp;type=3&amp;theater" target="_blank">wrote in the description of another photo</a>. "This is disgusting."</p>
<p>It's a museum-worthy meal for only three bucks and some change. That's not bad at all, if you don't mind some mild indigestion. </p>
