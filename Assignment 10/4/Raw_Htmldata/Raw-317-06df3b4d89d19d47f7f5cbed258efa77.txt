</h3>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-3LciNBlo_J0/Vrhy09IbsjI/AAAAAAAAPKs/kzrBQVwL6wY/s1600/%2540Walmart%2B%2523JennyCraigKit%2BAcorn%2BInfluence%2BCampaign%2BWeight%2BLoss%2BStarter%2BKit%2Bin%2Bbox%2BPhoto%2B1%2BWATERMARKED.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="Jenny Craig's new weight loss starter kits being sold exclusively at Walmart" border="0" height="266" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-3LciNBlo_J0/Vrhy09IbsjI/AAAAAAAAPKs/kzrBQVwL6wY/s400/%2540Walmart%2B%2523JennyCraigKit%2BAcorn%2BInfluence%2BCampaign%2BWeight%2BLoss%2BStarter%2BKit%2Bin%2Bbox%2BPhoto%2B1%2BWATERMARKED.jpg" title="Jenny Craig's new weight loss starter kits being sold exclusively at Walmart" width="400" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
If you checked out Friday's <b><u><a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/BBad6EQLyu3/?taken-by=ascending1" target="_blank"><span style="color: blue;">Instagram</span></a></u></b><span style="color: blue;">&nbsp;</span>and <b><u><a href="http://www.ascendingbutterfly.com/2016/02/jennycraigkit-now-available-walmart-ad.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: blue;">Blog Post</span></a></u></b> Teaser, then you know I am going to spill the tea today on Jenny Craig's new weight loss kits being sold exclusively at <b><u><a href="http://jennycraig.com/walmart" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: blue;">Walmart</span></a></u></b> in participating stores. (right now in store only)</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Last year I entered a blog contest over at <b><u><a href="http://thechattymomma.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: blue;">The Chatty Momma</span></a></u></b> for one month of Jenny Craig which included weekly one-on-one consultations with a dedicated consultant who would help identify challenges, map out goals and track my progress as well as a one month supply of food. Shout out to Tonia for the amazing giveaway, it really helped me get a good running start to re-vamping my Lifestyle Choices when it comes to meals and portion control.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
That was my first ever intro to Jenny Craig, I had never tried a program like this prior to this experience and I am happy to report I was successful at losing weight with the program - Members following the Jenny Craig program on average lose 1-2 lbs. a week.</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Out of the four weeks, I had only one 'un-successful' week and it is because I didn't stick with it, I was away from the meals most of that week because of my insane schedule - that is the only week I gained, not much, but it proved without a shadow of a doubt that if you stick to it, the program really does yield results. I wasn't just 'out and about' I was traveling so I couldn't take the food with me, if only this kit had been available then, I could have just purchased one when I landed!</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
As promised, I am going to prove to you that this <b><i>average sized box</i></b> actually contains 3 meals a day PLUS a snack for FIVE days (here is the kit 'un-boxed'):</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
