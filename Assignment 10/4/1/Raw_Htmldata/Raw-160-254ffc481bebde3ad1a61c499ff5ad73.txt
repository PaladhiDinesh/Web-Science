        <div id="article-body-content" itemprop="articleBody"> 
        	<p>Toronto police are looking for a man who got into a fight with another man on board a TTC bus.</p>
<p>The fight happened at Bathurst subway station around 6:15 a.m. on Jan. 24, police say.</p>
<p>Both of the men then got off the bus. Police found one of the men a short distance away. He had serious injuries.</p>
<p>They are now looking for the second man.</p>
<p>He is six-feet-tall, 175 pounds, with a medium build and short black hair. Police say he was wearing a black, hooded, bomber jacket, black pants, black running shoes and was carrying a black backpack.</p>
<p>Anyone with information is asked to contact police.</p>
