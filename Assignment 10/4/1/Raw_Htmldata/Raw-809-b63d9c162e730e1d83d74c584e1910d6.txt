			<h2 class="entry-title">New Hampshire shock Trifecta -Trump &#8211; Kasich-Christie ..Rubio/Cruz come up lame</h2>
			<p class="entry-meta">By <a href="http://ronrambles.com/author/Ron/" title="Posts by Ron" rel="author">Ron</a> on February 6, 2016</p>
<iframe src="http://www.facebook.com/plugins/like.php?href=http%3A%2F%2Fronrambles.com%2Fnew-hampshire-will-shake-up-the-gop-race-new-longshot-emerges%2F&amp;layout=standard&amp;show_faces=false&amp;width=450&amp;action=like&amp;colorscheme=light" scrolling="no" frameborder="0" allowTransparency="true" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; width:450px; height:60px; margin-top:15px;"></iframe>
			<div class="entry-content">
				
				<p>Look for Trump to take the top spot in New Hampshire primary. His winning margin won&#8217;t impress the mainstream media&#8230;because they work with the 6 media companies that control 90% of the news. Those conglomerates don&#8217;t want Trump..they are doing there best to portray his win as a loss.</p>
<p>Rubio had strong momentum out of Iowa&#8230;but his Saturday night debate debacle has hurt his credibility and mojo.  Here&#8217;s where things get interesting. Despite Ted Cruz strong Iowa performance he&#8217;s  sounding  like a one hit wonder. Based on current movement in the polls look for Cruz to fall to <strong>5th</strong> place.</p>
<p>Both John Kasick and Chris Christie have become  legitimate candidates . Both have strong resumes, experience and talent. Christie has guts. He&#8217;s the only adult that is willing to thoughtfully take on social security and entitlements. He wants to raise the retirement age gradually over the next 25 years. He also is being honest with the public the only way to make social security secure&#8230;we must institute MEANS TESTING. His plan would not pay social security benefits to wealthy individuals who have 200,000+ in retirement accounts. Almost all those folks are worth  millions of dollars. This type of means testing makes sense. Only Christie has the courage and intelligence to quarterback  this type of major overhaul.</p>
<p>So here we go, final predictions of the GOP New Hampshire primary</p>
<p>1. Donald Trump 27% &#8230;..solid win&#8230;front runner status</p>
<p>2. John Kasich    18% &#8230; upstart candidate on fire&#8230;Ohio resume boost chances</p>
<p>3. Chris Christie 15.%&#8230;working  hard&#8230; debating well&#8230;long shot with a chance.</p>
<p>4. Marco Rubio   14.%&#8230;major breakdown in debate&#8230;not ready for primetime</p>
<p>5. Ted Cruz         12.%&#8230; After  Iowa bump he hits a New Hampshire headwind</p>
<p>6.  Jeb Bush        11%.. decent debate performance keeps him in the race</p>
<p>Trump ,Kasich &amp; Christie winners in New Hampshire&#8230;.Rubio and Cruz big losers&#8230;</p>
<p>Odds to get the nomination</p>
<p>Trump 55%</p>
<p>Kasich 15%</p>
<p>Christie 10%</p>
<p>Rubio    10%</p>
<p>Cruz       5%</p>
<p>Bush    5%</p>
