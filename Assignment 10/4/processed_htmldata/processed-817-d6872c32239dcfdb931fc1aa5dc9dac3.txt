   Whatever you think about Donald J. Trump, one thing is for sure: the
   man is not flustered by hecklers.

   At a recent rally in Exeter, New Hampshire, the GOP frontrunner was
   heckled by a group of activists who shouted that illegal immigrants are
   "the backbone of this country." Trump's response was classic Donald.

   "I don't think so darling," Donald responded to the women in the crowd.
