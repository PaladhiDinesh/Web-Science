   Walmart announced it is closing 269 stores worldwide. Here are five
   things you need to know about the company. Ryan Connelly Holmes, USA
   TODAY
   USA TODAY 10:37 a.m. EST January 16, 2016
   AFP 535745237 A POL USA MO

   Walmart is closing 154 stores in the U.S.(Photo: AFP/Getty Images)
   21476 CONNECT[73]TWEET[74] 445 LINKEDIN 450 COMMENTEMAILMORE

   Walmart announced Friday that is closing 269 stores worldwide as it
   sharpens its focus on its supercenters and e-commerce business.

   In the United States, 154 stores will be closed, including: 102 Walmart
   Express stores, 6 discount centers, 12 Supercenters, 23 Neighborhood
   Markets, 4 Sam's Clubs and 7 Amigo stores in Puerto Rico.
