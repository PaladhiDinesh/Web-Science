BMS SUBWAY SPIRIT NIGHTS - Feb. 1, 8, & 15

   posted Feb 1, 2016, 7:09 AM by Jeanna Bryson

   [252][SUBWAY%20%20night%20feb.png?height=303&amp;width=400]
   Mark your calendars for BMS SUBWAY SPIRIT NIGHTS!!!
   The first 3 Mondays in February eat at Subway.
   Feb. 1st, 8th, & 15th, 2016
   We are actually having a competition between the grade levels, however
   you can come ALL 3 days.
   10% of the sales for the WHOLE DAY will go to BMS.
   You will get to see grade level BMS staff there from 5pm-8:00pm each
   night.
