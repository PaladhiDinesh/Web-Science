Frequently asked questions about VinylWritten

   [ ] Does this decal come in another color?
   YES! You may choose any of our 35 color options! At checkout, there
   will be a box where you can leave us a message. You can leave any
   personalization and color requests there.
   [ ] How do I know this decal will stick to my wall?
   We use the highest grade of quality vinyl on the market that is
   specifically made for interior walls or temporary applications. Our
   vinyl decals adhere best to smooth, clean and dry surfaces. Although
   very rare (less than 1%), adhesive failure can occur, however,
   sometimes it can be difficult to pinpoint the cause because every
   surface is different. Some reasons for failure can include:
   -texture of the wall
   -the type of paint used
   -not allowing the paint to cure long enough
   -humidity
   -dust
   If you are unsure if a decal will stick, please contact us prior to
   ordering and we will be happy to send you a sample. You may also order
   a sample pack and choose up to five colors here:
   https://www.etsy.com/listing/223075651
   [ ] I am painting my room this weekend. Can I put up the decal right
   away?
   We recommend waiting a minimum of three weeks to allow the paint to
   cure. As paint cures, it emits vapors. These vapors can get trapped
   under the vinyl and create bubbles, ultimately causing your decal to
   fall off the wall.
   [ ] I'm not sure what color I should choose-Help!
   We have great news! You can try before you buy and order a sample pack
   and choose up to five colors, ensuring you pick the best colors to
   coordinate with your room. The sample pack can be ordered here:
   https://www.etsy.com/listing/22307565
   [ ] I paid extra for shipping. When will I get my order?
   Our current production time is 3-5 business days, but we try to get
   every order out before then. If you upgrade your shipping to Priority
   Mail, this does not change the production time-it only reduces the
   amount of time in transit through the postal system. Most orders are
   delivered in 1-3 business days after they are scanned at the post
   office, however, that is only an estimate and not guaranteed. If you
   need your order guaranteed by a certain date, we can ship via USPS
   Express Mail and that usually adds aprox $25 to the shipping cost.
   [ ] I just realized my shipping address is incorrect. What happens now?
   We print all of our shipping labels directly through the Etsy shipping
   system. Your label will be printed EXACTLY as you entered it in Etsy.
   If we have not yet shipped your item, just send us an Etsy Convo and
   let us know of the new address. Orders that have already been shipped
   will be handled on a case by case basis. It may take longer for your
   order to be forwarded to you or it may be returned to us. If your
   package is returned to us due to incorrect or insufficient address
   info, you will be asked to purchase a new label before we can ship it
   again.
