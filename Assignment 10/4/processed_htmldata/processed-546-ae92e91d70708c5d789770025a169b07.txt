Walmart Price Match Recipe: Pizza Pasta Bake

   Sometimes you come home after a long day and simply want something
   warm, cheesy and most importantly easy to make.  This weeks Walmart
   Price Match Recipe Pizza Pasta Bake is just the thing to make.  Not
   only is it fast to throw together but it has all the things your family
   will love.  Pasta, pepperoni, pasta sauce and of course cheese.  This
   recipe is a great one to throw together last minute.  We keep most of
   the ingredients on hand in our pantry at all times because it is so
   easy to coupon for them.

   Walmart Price Match Recipe: Pizza Pasta Bake
   Serves: 8

   Ingredients
     * 6 ounces Pepperoni
     * 12 oz. Pasta of Choice (we prefer Rigatoni as it holds sauce well)
     * 20oz Jar Pasta Sauce of Choice
     * 2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, divided
     * Additional "pizza" toppings as desired such as olives, bell pepper,
       onion or mushrooms

   Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
    2. Spray 9x13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray and set aside
    3. Cook Pasta as directed on package, drain and pour back into pan.
    4. Mix in pasta sauce, pepperoni and 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
       along with any other "pizza" toppings preferred.
    5. Pour into prepared baking dish and top with remaining cheese.
    6. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or until heated through and
       cheese has melted and begun to bubble and brown.

   3.2.1303


   Not only will this Pizza Pasta Bake be a great easy meal for you to mix
   up, it is cost effective feeding a family of 4 for 2 meals for around
   $5-$6 or less depending on sales and coupons.  Customize it to your own
   personal tastes with varied toppings or go with the simple pepperoni
   pizza taste that kids love.
